Create personalized videos in minutes with automation

AutoVid Generator transforms your data into dynamic videos using Google Sheets, Gemini, Pexels, and FFmpeg, simplifying content creation and unleashing your creativity.

The problem we solve

Manual video creation is slow, requires advanced technical skills, and can limit creativity. Current tools are complex, expensive, or do not allow large-scale automated personalization.

Our solution (MVP)

AutoVid Generator automates video creation: it pulls data from Google Sheets, generates content with AI (Gemini, DeepL), produces text-to-speech audio, sources visuals from Pexels, and processes videos with FFmpeg for professional results.
